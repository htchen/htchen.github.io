Evaluation Data for User Study

The following are 626 sets of evaluation data for user study.
Each set contains three images (from left to right): the randomly initialized view, the suggested view produced by our algorithm, and the referenced exemplar.
The exemplar collection consists of beautiful landscape and outdoor photos taken by professional photographers, including Ansel Adams, Jay Dickman, Peter Essick, and William Neill.
